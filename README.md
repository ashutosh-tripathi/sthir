# STHIR

**Sthir** (स्थिर) is a minimalist activity-tracking app designed to help you build consistency through intentional action.  
Create activities, schedule them daily, and manually start and finish them — without automatic monitoring or distractions.

---

## What is STHIR?

STHIR is a manual-first activity tracker that focuses on **clarity, presence, and steady progress**.  
Instead of passively tracking everything, STHIR encourages you to consciously begin and complete activities.

---

## Features

### Create Activities
- Create custom activities such as workouts, reading, learning, or focus sessions
- Assign colors and daily goal durations to each activity
- Keep activities flexible and personal

---

### Schedule Your Day
- Schedule activities for specific days or every day
- View all planned activities in a clean daily timeline
- Use scheduling as a way to set intention, not pressure

---

### Start & Finish Activities
- Start activities manually with a single tap
- Track time using a simple stopwatch-style interface
- Finish activities when you’re done — no background tracking

---

### Progress Rings
- Track daily progress using ring-based visual indicators
- Rings fill as you complete time or sessions
- Rings reset every day to encourage fresh starts

---

### Daily Overview
- View completed and pending activities at a glance
- Understand how your day was spent without complex analytics
- Focus on completion, not optimization

---

## Design & Experience

- Inspired by Apple Fitness UI
- Minimal, distraction-free interface
- Optimized for dark mode
- Designed for quick glances and intentional interaction

---

## Platform Support

### Currently
- Web app (desktop and mobile browsers)
- Optimized for iPhone-sized screens

### Planned
- Native iPhone app
- Apple Watch companion app
- Apple Calendar integration
- Optional iCloud sync

---

## What STHIR Does Not Do

- No automatic activity detection
- No health or sensor data tracking
- No ads
- No social feeds or leaderboards

---

## Who Is STHIR For?

- People building daily habits
- Professionals tracking focus and deep work
- Users who prefer manual, mindful tracking
- Anyone who values simplicity over metrics

---

## Philosophy

STHIR is not about doing more.  
It’s about **showing up, steadily**.
